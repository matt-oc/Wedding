Accommodation Info

Ballykealey House can accommodate 109 guests on site, between the 12 rooms in the Manor House and 15 self-catering lodges.


This accommodation will be booked/allocated through us so please get in touch if you wish to stay on site and we will do our best to accommodate you here.


One contact number per Bedroom is required. All these guests will be contacted by Ballykealey House and asked for an email address to send them a booking confirmation for their reservation and a debit/credit card number to secure their room. No deposits or upfront payments are taken however all guests Pre-Pay for their rooms at least 14 days in advance. Ballykealey will then charge the card guests give and email a receipt and give the Check-In information.


Prices below for the lodges and rooms (breakfast is included for all):

  • €180.00 Double Room Based on 2 adults sharing 1 night
  • €310.00 Lodge Based on 4 guests sharing 1 night (lodges have 2 bedrooms, sitting room and full kitchen)
  • Some lodges sleep 5 people, there is an additional charge of €60 for the 5th person.

Unfortunately, Ballykealey House cannot accommodate all guest on site. However, we have attached a list of alternative accommodation within a 10/15 minute taxi drive away! We have 10 rooms set aside in Mount Wolseley Hotel, with a special wedding rate! Please quote our names when booking to get a special rate of €249.00 B&B, per Double Deluxe room, for two guests sharing.

Any rooms not booked by our guests 6 weeks prior to the wedding date will be released to public sales. So please book as soon as possible if you wish to stay here.
